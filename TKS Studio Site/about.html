About

Welcome to my photography about page! My name is Nonthokozo Nxumalo, and I'm a South African-based photographer specializing in Stance Photography. I've been passionate about photography for three years, and I love to capture unique and meaningful moments through my lens.


My journey as a photographer started when I had interest in cars, the interior of the cars and more. Since then, I've honed my skills and developed my own style, which can be described as Aesthwhen Vintage Style. I'm constantly striving to push my boundaries and challenge myself creatively to produce the best possible work.


My work has been featured in Speed magazine, Speed Freak page and I've been fortunate enough to work with a diverse range of clients from Stance Exhibitions. Whether it's capturing the joy and excitement of a wedding, the natural beauty of a landscape, or the personality of a model, I always aim to tell a story through my photos.


When you work with me, you can expect a personalized experience tailored to your needs. I take the time to understand what you're looking for, and I work closely with you to ensure that we achieve the results you want. Whether it's a portrait session, event coverage, or commercial work, I approach every project with enthusiasm and a commitment to excellence.


In addition to my photography services, I also offer Professional editing, so feel free to reach out if you're interested in those as well.


Thank you for taking the time to visit my photography about page. If you have any questions or would like to schedule a session, please don't hesitate to contact me. I look forward to hearing from you!
